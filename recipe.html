Triane's Magical Chocolate Chunk Toffee Nut Cookies

Description

My favorite CBD infused cookies for a great night time snack. Replace with regular butter if you would like standard cookies.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup (2 sticks) Cannabis Butter or ¾ cup canna butter and 1¼ unsalted for low tolerance
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • ¾ teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1 cup(packed) dark brown sugar
  • ⅓ cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs, room temperature
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup Toffee Nut
  • 1½ cups (2 bars)Chocolate bars(perferably dark chocolate) cut into chunks
  • Flaky sea salt

Directions

  1. Cook butter in a medium saucepan over medium heat, stirring often, until it foams, then browns, takes about 5–8 minutes. Scrape into a large heat safe bowl and let cool slightly, until cool enough to touch (like the temperature of a warm bath), takes about about 10 minutes.

  2. Meanwhile, whisk sifted flour, baking soda, and kosher salt in a medium bowl.

  3. Add brown sugar and granulated sugar to browned butter. Beat until incorporated. Add eggs and vanilla, increase and beat until mixture and begins to thicken. Slowly add dry ingredients. Beat just to combine. Mix in toffee pieces and chocolate. Let dough sit at room temperature at least 30 minutes for best results let rest for 1 to 2 days Dough will thicken as it sits.

  4. When ready, to cook Preheat to 375°. Using a 1-oz. ice cream scoop or spoon portion out balls of dough and place on a buttered (of your choice)baking sheet, spacing about 3" apart (you can also form dough into ping pong–sized balls with your hands). Do not flatten; cookies will spread as they bake. Sprinkle with sea salt.

  5. Bake cookies until edges are golden brown and firm but centers are still soft, 9–11 minutes. Let cool.
